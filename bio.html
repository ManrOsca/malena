Bio




I am a UK based storyteller but a storyteller with a camera. My personnal works have been published and have won several awards. I hope my images and discoveries revive experiences and foster future hope.

In 2001 I successfully applied for and received a grant from the Spanish Ministry of Culture; this grant enabled the publication of my book of photographs titled “Boca del Cielo”. Earlier in 2000 my work “Boca del Cielo” was shown in a solo exhibition in Alcoy and then in 2001, was shown in Sueca, Spain. in 2008 I was asked to take part in the group exhibition “Visiones de Mexico” 21 fotografos (Valencia); this was curated by the renowned photographer Bernard Plossu with the catalogue edited by the University of Valencia. In 2013 part of the exhibition “Boca del Cielo” was shown on the walls of the Gallery Take Away, Reading, UK.

EXHIBITIONS

I have been proud to take part in many group shows such as the “Museo Alternativo (where? 1996); Cinco anos de ciclo experimental de fotografia de Valencia (Valencia 2000) “Jocs Florals a Cavanilles (Valencia 2005) and in 2008 I was asked to take part in the group exhibition “Visiones de Mexico” 21 fotografos (Valencia); this was curated by the renowned photographer Bernard Plossu. In 2010 I participated in the exhibition “Donart Gandia” (Borja Palace, Gandia), a group exhibition by female artists. In 2010 I also had a picture selected from the “Hola Elena”for the visual arts competition (Coll Alas Gallery, Gandia)

My solo exhibitions include “La Marina”(1998-1999) which was exhibited in two Spanish cities (where?) and “Vora Riu” (2007)a project selected for the Descubrimientos PhotoEspaña 2003 (?) programme. In 2007 this project was shown at the UNESCO Centre, in my home town, Alcoy, Spain. Earlier in 2000 my work “Boco del Cielo” was shown in a solo exhibition again in Alcoy and then in 2001 was shown in Sueca, Spain. In 2013 part of the exhibition “Boca del Cielo” was shown on the walls of the Gallery Take Away, Reading, UK.

In April 2008 I presented the exhibition “Return Tickets” at the Space 2410 gallery in Wakayama, Japan.

Also in 2008-2009 the “Solo de ida” and “Aish” projects were exhibited in Gandia, Spain and the series of photographs “Aish” were shown in a solo exhibition at the Egyptian Institute in Madrid, Spain. In 2013, my exhibition “Hola Elena” was shown at the Harvey Gallery in Guildford, U.K
