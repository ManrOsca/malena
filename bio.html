  1. Bio

:w

Elena Garcia-Juan is a UK based. She is a storyteller with a camera. Elena’s personal works have won several awards and been published. Her images revive experiences and discovers from her travels. Her professional work includes from press photography to high-end retouching, through qualified instructor in adult education institutions. Elena has taught and influenced works of several photographers with her friendly advice.
