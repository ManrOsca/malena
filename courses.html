I love to share my knowledge and transmit/transfer my passion for photography with my students.
After running evening courses for two years in Popular University in Gandia. Spain, I started working for Adult Learning Center in Surrey when I relocated in the U.K. I’ve been building up my confidence in teaching digital photography. From basic courses to advanced learners, I had been a curator for a couple of successful exhibitions of my students. I actually work for WEA in the Southern area and I run workshops, one to one and evening courses for anyone who wants to approach to digital photography with any camera/device.
